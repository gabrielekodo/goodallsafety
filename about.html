OUR VALUES

Goodall Safety Consultants Ltd (Goodall Safety) is a Ugandan based privately owned company that provides a broad range of radiation safety services mainly for regulatory compliance in radiology units of the medical sector, Non-Destructive Testing sector and relevant sectors that might need these services.

Goodall Safety was established in 2022 recognized and reserved with the UNBS under number R2000005909310. The company was established with the aim of helping you to take advantages of its expertise and experience in the Ionising Radiation sector of Uganda to ensure that you fully comply to the national and international requirements for radiation safety for radiation workers, public and the environment.

Given our experience in this business, be rest assured that our staff will provide the most efficient, reliable and economical solutions and services meet national and international standards.

Our vision and ultimate goal is being East Africa’s preferred and most reliable radiation safety service provider.
